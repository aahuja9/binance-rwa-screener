Binance TradFi perpetuals — volume & open-interest momentum

Note: the previous 24h window overlaps a weekend. Underlying stock markets were closed, so volume-change numbers are inflated for equities. Open-interest change is unaffected.

Vol windows: last 24 closed hourly candles vs the 24 before. OI from hourly openInterest history (notional USDT). Vol/OI = 24h volume divided by current OI (turnover). Div = whether the underlying pays a dividend (curated static data, hover for details, click for history; "?" = Binance alias ticker, unverified). Perp holders never receive dividends; Binance neutralizes them via index price adjustment on ex-div dates.

Connection settings

Data is fetched directly from fapi.binance.com in your browser. If requests fail (HTTP 451 geo-block or CORS), set a proxy prefix. The full Binance URL is appended, URL-encoded, to the prefix.
